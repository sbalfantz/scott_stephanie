Travel & Accomodations

We’re incredibly grateful to have loved ones traveling from near and far to celebrate with us—it means the world! For those flying in, the closest airport is Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG).

Transportation

Rent a Car

This may be the best option so you can easily get to the hotel, explore on your extra days here, and get to the welcome party!

Ride Share

Most ride shares are pretty close to the airport, but the hotel is about 35/40 min away from there. May be harder to get to the welcome party but not impossible!

Shuttle

We will have a shuttle/bus leaving the hotel around 4:30 and making trips to and from the venue with the last trip leaving at 5:15. They will then be going from the venue to the hotel starting at 10 and the last shuttle will go at 11:30 if needed.
