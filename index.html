All Thanks to the Hanks

It all started with a babysitting gig and a little matchmaking magic! When Stephanie’s roommate Izzy introduced her to Caleb, Kaylee, and their son Carter, no one knew that a simple night of helping out would lead to something extraordinary. But Caleb and Kaylee saw a spark waiting to happen—and they knew Scott had to meet Stephanie.

So, over a home-cooked meal at the Hanks’ house, Scott and Stephanie finally crossed paths. That night was the beginning of countless adventures—many trips to Austin, Fort Worth, Kentucky, and the Ranch, laughter-filled weekends, and memories that stitched our lives together.

We’re endlessly grateful to the Hanks for their friendship and intuition. Without them, we might still be wandering the world, searching for the love we found in each other.
